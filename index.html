Hi, I'm Jenn!

I'm a PhD student in computational neuroscience at the Center for Neural Science at New York University. Starting Decmeber 2019, I'll be working in AI ethics as a policy analyst with the Government of Canada.

I read broadly within ethical theory, AI, consciousness, philosophy of mind, neurophenomenology, philosophy of science, and the Indigenous sciences.

I am a proponent of radical science; that is, I support the conception of science as a constitutively social endeavour which ought be by and for the people.

Lastly, I believe in political strength through neighbourly community-building. Since 2016, I've helped build the Scientist Action and Advocacy Network (ScAAN), an organization of New York-based scientists who provide pro bono research to partner NGOs. I'm here to make friends! Let's chat...
