jenn

I'm an Ottawa-based policy analyst in AI ethics, simultaneously completing a PhD in computational neuroscience in the Ma Lab at New York University. Like many others, I believe in the possibility of a science for the people. I support decentralized, neighbourly community-building, activism, and policy-making to that end.

This is a hybrid personal-professional site. I'll post both informal and academic writing, as well as community resources for scientists, workers, and activists. I'm broadly interested in neuroscience, neuroethics, philosophy of mind, Indigenous sciences, and the impact of technology on labour. I'm also slowly learning to name trees in my spare time!

Please drop me a line if you need a friend or ally. Who doesn't? :) CV available on request.
