hi, i'm jenn!

I'm a policy analyst in AI ethics, currently working in Ottawa, Canada. I'm simultaneously a PhD student in computational neuroscience in the Ma Lab at New York University.

Like many others, I envision a science for the people, and support neighbourly community-building, activism, and policy to that end.

This site includes some of my academic and non-academic articles. It also contains links to resources for scientists, policy analysts, and activists.

I am mainly interested in computational neuroscience, AI ethics, AI and labour, and neuroethics. I am also always trying to learn more about philosophy of science, philosophy of mind, and neurophenomenology.

I'm here to make friends! Please drop me a line.
